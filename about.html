About Us

Welcome to R&R Hash House, where we serve a delightful variety of dishes made from the freshest ingredients. Our chefs are dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience with exceptional service.

Founded in 2024, our restaurant has quickly become a favorite in the community, known for our cozy atmosphere and delicious food. Whether you're here for a family dinner or a romantic evening, we have something for everyone.

Meet Our Talented Chefs

Our culinary team is led by Chef Saransh Goila, a seasoned professional with over 15 years of experience in the restaurant industry. He has worked in some of the finest kitchens around the world and brings his expertise to every dish we serve. Chef Doe's passion for food and commitment to quality is what sets R&R Hash House apart.

Chef Saransh Goila (Left) and Chef Aditi Dey (Right) in the kitchen, where they create mouthwatering meals using the finest ingredients.

Chef Aditi Dey, who leads our pastry section, is a renowned baker with multiple awards to her name. Her creative pastries and desserts have become a fan favorite at R&R Hash House.

Achievements

  • Our chefs have also been featured on several popular food TV shows.

At R&R Hash House, we are proud of the hard work and dedication of our chefs, and we strive to provide a memorable dining experience with every meal.
